FAQ - Movies and Where to Watch

1. What is TMDB?

The Movie Database (TMDB) is an online database with detailed information about movies, TV shows, and artists. It offers a vast collection of data, including synopses, trailers, images, and much more.

2. How is the movie list updated?

The movie list on our site is automatically updated based on the latest information provided by TMDB.

3. How can I find where to watch a specific movie?

On each movie's detail page, you'll find a section indicating the streaming platforms where the movie is available.

4. Do I need to create an account to use the site?

You do not need to create an account to view the movie list.

5. What should I do if I find an error in the movie information?

If you find an error in the movie information, please contact us through the contact section or send an email to contact@bazinga.com

6. Can I suggest new movies to add to the site?

All information and movies are automatically pulled from TMDB. If you want to suggest new content, the best way is to add the data directly on TMDB, which will then be reflected on our site.
